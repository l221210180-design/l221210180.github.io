About Our Laboratory

This platform provides students and teachers with essential information regarding laboratory safety, proper equipment handling, laboratory rules, and facility care. It is available 24/7 and promotes a paperless educational environment.

🛡 Laboratory Safety Rules

🥼 Wear PPE

Always use lab coats, gloves, and safety goggles when required.

🚫 No Food

Eating and drinking inside the laboratory is prohibited.

⚠ Follow Instructions

Always follow teacher and laboratory supervisor instructions.

🚨 Report Accidents

Immediately report spills, accidents, or damaged equipment.

🔬 Proper Use of Equipment

Microscopes

Handle carefully and store properly after use.

Glassware

Inspect laboratory glassware before conducting experiments.

Balances

Use precision equipment responsibly and keep it clean.

Chemicals

Follow all handling and storage procedures for chemical substances.

🏢 Care of Laboratory Facilities

Clean Work Areas

Leave workstations clean and organized after every practice.

Protect Furniture

Avoid damaging tables, chairs, cabinets, and laboratory infrastructure.

Waste Disposal

Dispose of laboratory waste according to established procedures.

Environmental Care

Promote sustainability through responsible resource usage.
